Three shipped projects — an internship platform, a final year project, and a client system. Each page tells the full story: the problem, what I built, and why I built it that way.

Internship · Syarikat Tajau Berseri 2026 Sole developer

SmartTaxApp

Malaysian businesses don't know what SST tax to charge — so most guess, and get it wrong. I built a compliance platform that resolves the right tax from database rules, isolates every tenant's data with PostgreSQL Row-Level Security, and improves its own classification data every Monday without a developer touching it.

Final Year Project · Swinburne Sarawak 2025–2026 Navigation, AI & security lead

Swinburne Campus App

Students, parents, and visitors kept getting lost on a growing campus. Our team surveyed students, then built what they asked for: 360° navigation as the core feature, an AI assistant that knows when not to answer, events, funding info, and profiles — validated with 30+ functional, security, and load tests.

Client project · Semangoh Wildlife Centre 2025–2026 IoT + ML + App

Semangoh Park Guard System

A wildlife centre with endangered plants and no early-warning system for intrusions. We walked the rainforest zones first, then built ESP32 sensor devices with clean one-event-one-record alerting, a real-time ranger dashboard, and an AI model that identifies rare plants from a phone camera.
